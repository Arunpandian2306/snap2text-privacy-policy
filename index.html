Snap2Text

Snap2Text (“we”, “our”, or “the App”) is developed and operated by PairBridge India Pvt Ltd. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring your data is handled safely.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Image Input (OCR)

When you use the Image-to-Text feature, the image you select is processed only for OCR extraction. We do not store, upload, or share your images or extracted text.

1.2 QR Code Features

• QR Code Scanner: Processes data locally on your device.
• QR Code Generator: Generates QR codes based on the text you provide.
We do not collect, store, or transmit this information.

1.3 App Usage Data

We may collect anonymous analytics (such as crash logs or feature usage) to improve the app. This data does not identify you personally.

2. How We Use Information

We use collected information only to:

  • Improve app performance
  • Enhance user experience
  • Fix bugs or crashes

3. No Data Sharing

We do not sell, share, or transfer your personal data to third parties. All processing happens securely on your device unless explicitly stated.

4. Children’s Privacy

Snap2Text does not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

5. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information. As no method of electronic storage is 100% secure, we recommend avoiding sensitive data.
